In order to install the dupe checker, please do the following


1-Install forge 1.8.9

2-Remove any other mods from the mod folder since it can interfere

3-Launch Minecraft and go to Skyblock

4-Use Y (default*) for GUI or do /dpchecker

5-A message will appear in chat saying:
	[DupeChecker] Item is not duped!
	or
	[DupeChecker] WARNING Item is duped!







*The keybind can be changed in controls
